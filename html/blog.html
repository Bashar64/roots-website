The Top 3 Shipping Problems for Small Businesses in Jordan (And How to Fix Them)

Whether you are selling via Instagram, Zid, Salla, or looking for reliable Shopify shipping Jordan, you have likely faced these common challenges:

1. Managing "Cash on Delivery" (COD) Collections
In the MENA region, over 70% of online shoppers prefer Cash on Delivery. For store owners, this means you don't get paid until the package is handed over. Slow couriers mean slow cash flow.
The Fix: Partner with a logistics company that guarantees fast dispatch and automated cash reconciliation, so your money hits your account faster.

2. Slow Delivery Times Ruining the Customer Experience
When someone buys online, they want it immediately. If your current shipping provider takes 3 to 4 days for a delivery inside Amman, your customers will get frustrated, cancel the order, or buy from a competitor.
The Fix: Use a local fulfillment center in Jordan that offers guaranteed 24-hour hyperlocal delivery. Fast delivery builds trust and turns one-time buyers into loyal, repeat customers.

3. Running Out of Space (The Inventory Nightmare)
As your small business grows, storing products in your house or a small rented office becomes impossible. Picking and packing boxes until 2:00 AM leads to burnout and packing mistakes.
The Fix: This is where e-commerce fulfillment comes in. By moving your products to a professional warehouse, you eliminate the need to pack boxes ever again.
